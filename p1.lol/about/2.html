About me (2)

Since late 2025, I live in Bavaria, working for a production company. Oh, and I'm also a more or less active member of the Chaos Computer Club, often found in hackspaces around Munich and Nuremberg.
I collect CDs, vinyl records, retro tech gadgets, and old cameras. I listen to a lot of experimental stuff, such as Death Industrial, Dark Ambient, but also Rock, Metal, Pop Punk, 80s and earlier. (A very few of) my favorite bands and artists are Ada Rook, Cremation Lily, Uboa, HEALTH, My Chemical Romance, Green Day, Foo Fighters, Evanescence and many many more. This is my bandcamp collection.
