Who am I?

My name is Schokopuddingg, born in 2006, and I've been a tech geek for as long as I can remember. Even as a kid, I was fascinated by old devices — cameras, tape recorders, broadcast gear, and anything that blinks, beeps, or buzzes.

Today, I'm deeply into broadcast and video technology, radio, smart home systems, and both analog and digital photography. I document many of my projects on YouTube, Twitch, and my archive portal broadcastarchiv.de (German).

In 2023 and 2024, I attended the Maker Faire Hannover as an exhibitor, running a fully functional TV studio together with friends.

Since late 2025, I live in Bavaria, working for a production company. Oh, and I'm also a more or less active member of the Chaos Computer Club.

I collect CDs, vinyl records, retro tech gadgets, and old cameras. I listen to a lot of Rock, Metal, Pop Punk, but also 80s and earlier. (A very few of) my favorite bands are Lorna Shore, My Chemical Romance, Green Day, Foo Fighters, Evanescence and many more.
