About Me

Hey! I'm Schokopuddingg, and I'm obsessed with bits, bytes, pixels, grain and waves. I absolutely love tinkering with tech, whether it's vintage computers or tech gadgets from the 2000s. When I'm not geeking out over broadcast technology or photography, you can find me sharing my projects on YouTube Twitch, my other sites or the fediverse.

What you are seeing right now is one of my firts attempts creating a 2000s website purely with HTML and CSS.

Why? Because I absolutely love the web design style of that era and want to bring back some nostalgia!

What you will find here

Have a look around and find out for yourself! Just don't expect too much.

As time goes by, I'll try to add more content and maybe even some cool features to make this website a little y2k comfy place on the web.

But why y2k?

Sometimes I wish I would have experienced the world as a teenager back in the late 2000s. When Emos and Scene Kids were hanging out in the schoolyard, listening to My Chemical Romance, everyone had a Discman and a Flip Phone, and the internet was this mysterious place full of Geocities sites and flashing GIFs. I absolutely adore that era of Windows Vista Laptops, Skype calls and expensive mobile data plans. The y2k web design style has a unique charm that I find fascinating - it's colorful, playful, and full of personality. Plus, it's a fun challenge to recreate that aesthetic using just HTML and CSS.

So, why not embrace the nostalgia and have some fun with it?
